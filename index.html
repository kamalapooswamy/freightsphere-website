Time to re-think freight management

FreightSphere is redefining the way freight forwarders manage their daily operations. It puts modern technology with intelligent analytics and other powerful features in the hands of freight forwarders. We think there is no other solution like it!

End-to-End Freight Operations

FreightSphere supports end-to-end operations with a comprehensive set of features that include master data, rate contracts & rate cards, AWB workflows and execution, invoicing, payments, staff management, locations, and whole lot more.

Powerful Analytics

Powerful set of analytics that allow you to stay on top of the big picture and make right decisions quickly. You get to analyze and identify gaps in your rate cards and find opportunities to streamline strategies in picking right airlines, flights, schedules, and so on.

Cloud-based and 24X7 Availability

FreightSphere is a completely cloud-based solution which runs on a world-class cloud-platform from Microsoft. There is no need of any setup or hardware. This allows our customers to focus on growing their business while we focus on delivering the best possible tools to support their operations and decision-making process.

Top-Notch Security

FreightSphere runs on a cloud platform inside a highly secure and well-protected data center of Microsoft. In addition, FreightSphere uses 128-bit SSL, encryption algorithms, and other methods to protect customer data at all times. FreightSphere is certified by McAfee (a renowned security company) as a secure online service.

Awesome Support

We offer varied set of support tools with very helpful support staff. Our customers can email us, raise online tickets, go through FAQs, articles, and forums. They can call us or chat with us. We have made sure that our customers can get necessary help whenever they need it. By the way, we are also active on social media.
